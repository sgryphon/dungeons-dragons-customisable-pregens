Dungeons & Dragons Customisable Pre-Generated Characters

Creating a Dungeons & Dragons character can be a time consuming process, with dozens of decisions and hundreds of options to choose from.

For a quick start pre-generated characters are available, where all options have already been chosen.

These sheets provide a middle path, with a set of modular pre-generated characters, where players have an opportunity for customisation without being overwhelemed by choice.

Instructions

  1. Pick one Species sheet, one Background sheet, one Class sheet (some spell casters may have an extra sheet).
  2. Pick any options needed on your Species and Background sheets (many don't have any options).
  3. Copy in the Ability Score modifiers into the Skills on your Background and calculate the totals (adding +2 proficiency).
  4. Calculate your attack total for any weapons from your Background, adding the listed ability modifier plus, if you are proficient, add +2.
  5. On your Class sheet, pick your Skills and calculate the totals (adding modifier +2 proficiency).
  6. Pick your Personality and Alignment traits as a guide for how to portray your character. The approach and morality you pick determine your alignment.
  7. Select any Class Features that have options.
  8. Adjust any totals as necessary, e.g. if your background, species, or features change any values.
  9. Name your character, and you are ready to go.
